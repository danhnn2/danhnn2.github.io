Welcome to this Narrative

The Olympic Games are the leading international sporting events in which thousands of athletes from around the world participate in a variety of competitions

We, as a country, are well represented in the Olympics. The number of Olympics medals that we have accumulated over the years is, simply, astonishing.

However, the popularity the Olympics in the U.S. has steadily decreased over the years. Nowadays, the Olympics is, sadly, nothing but evening entertainment for most people.

This narrative aims to show you, the viewer, that the number of medals is not an effective measurement of Olympics popularity in the USA.
